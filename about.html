About the creator

My name is Patrizia and I have been interested in web design and development from the moment I started navigating the internet.

I started building HTML webpages a long time ago at the age of 16, when many of today's techniques were not yet developed and CSS was brand new!

I graduated in Multimedia Communications and Technologies at my hometown's University with the goal in mind to become a professional web developer, but due to a series of circumstances it wasn't meant to be at the time.

I moved to Ireland from Italy and, after 12 years, the current pandemic arrived. I was out of work for a while, and I decided that I wanted to see if I was able to go back to the roots of my passions and my studies.

Scouring the web, I found Udacity and the Front-End developer course, and I decided to enroll straight away.

I have found that, although many things have changed in the web development world since the last time I visited, the foundations remain, and the course has helped me so far to refresh my knowledge and learn about what changed in the meantime!

I can't wait to see what the rest of the course has in store for me and I am looking forward to being able to make a career out of my passion.

Italian expat in Ireland, rock and metal music fan, long time gamer and technology enthusiast. Want to learn more?
